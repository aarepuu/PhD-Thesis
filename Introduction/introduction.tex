\begin{enumerate}
  \item What is the level of engagement with data and literacy around using data that local
  \item What does it mean to participate in civic action through data sharing and use? a. What are the different ways of enabling data-driven participation? b. What tools are appropriate for collaborative sensemaking of data?
  \item What is the role of data in civic participation and advocacy?
a. Does the process of citizen sensemaking and giving people voices through data shift the power relationship between the publics and decision-makers, and strengthen the community?
